HEALTHY, LOCAL AND EASY!

Middags Huset makes real Norwegian healthy dinners that you order weekly on our app, select daytime when you want to pick up your dinner - and the best part is that you can pick it up in your neighbourhood! This saves you time. And your family gets healthy and good dinners - every day!

General info

Middags Huset provides ability to order meals in Norway for every afternoon of the next week until 17:00 Friday this week.

The schedule for 4 days (Monday - Thirsday) is displayed on the Home screen and shows the order-per-day information. Tap on a day leads to the corresponding Menu screen.

The menu contains 2 dishes - first and second courses. Tap on a dish box leads to the Order screen.

On the Order screen, the user can select the number of portions (from 1 to 9.5), specify convenient pick-up daytime, edit the order, confirm the order.
