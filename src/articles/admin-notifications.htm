In order to send notifications to customers, navigate to Promotions > Push Notifications.

The Push Notifications page appears:

Perform the following steps to notify users:

  • Click the Customers field and seelect users from the drop-down list, or select the the Select all customers checkbox to notify all users.

  • Select a newsletter template from the Templates drop-down list. In this case, all the further fields will be populated with the template data. Otherwise, leave this field untouched and proceed to the next steps

  • Specify the Title of the notification.

  • Specify the Subtitle of the notification

  • Enter the notification message

  • Click Send Notifications

Now, the notification is send to all the selected users and appears on their devices.
