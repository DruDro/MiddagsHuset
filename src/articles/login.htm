After the application launches, the Log in screen appears. Here you can perform one of the following:

  • Log in to Fixlist using your existing account. Provide your Login and Password:

  • Log in using your Facebook account: tap the f icon to use the Facebook app installed on your device or the facebook website, in order to grant access to your facebook credentials.

  • Create a new Fixlist profile: tap Registration and provide the following info:

    • Email Address required field — '@' and '.' are required characters, maximum - 256 characters

    • Password required field — 6 characters or more ( minimum 5 any symbols and at least 1 number), maximum - 256 characters

    • Duplicate your password required field

  • When ready, tap Log in, Continue as <Username>, Register respectively.

Next you get to the Home Screen. Here are presented active and archived Fixlist photos. By default, it shows active photos in an active fixlist. This will be described in details in Add a Photo and Create a Fixlist articles.
