After the application launches, the Log in screen appears. Here you can perform one of the following:

You can skip the following steps directly to the Home screen. Tap SE MENYEN / SEE MENU. You will be able to navigate through the application, but you can't make orders. To order dinners, the user must be logged in. You will be prompted to login or create a new account when trying to make an order. Moreover, new registered users get an unrivalled opportunity to make orders with a 50% discount during 24 hours. If the discount is active, you will see the corresponding timers on the app pages:

  • Log in to Middags Huset using your existing account. Provide your e-mail / din e-post and Password / Passord:

    and tap LOGG INN / LOG IN

  • Log in using your Facebook account: tap the FACEBOOK BUTTON to use the Facebook app installed on your device or the facebook website, in order to grant access to your facebook credentials and log in.

  • Create a new account: tap BLI MEDLEM / BECOME MEMBER and provide the following info:

    • E-post / Email Address required field — '@' and '.' are required characters, maximum - 256 characters

    • Passord / Password required field — 5 symbols or more, maximum - 100 symbols. Can't contain spaces only

    • Fornavn / Firstname required field — at least 5 symbols, maximum - 100 symbols

    • Etternavn / Lastname required field — at least 5 symbols, maximum - 100 symbols

    • Land / Country required field

    • By / City required field

    • Postnummer / Zip-code required field

    • Mobile / Phone optional field

    • Tap BLI MEDLEM / BECOME MEMBER

    • On the next screen, select the pick-up address:

      • By / City

      • Adresse / Address

    • Tap SE MENYEN / SEE MENU
