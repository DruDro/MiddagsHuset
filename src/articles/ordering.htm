When logged in, you are able to order dinners for the next week.

The only condition is that your previous (week) order is already paid.

To make an order, from the Home screen follow the steps below:

  • Select the Week / Uke you want to make an order for. At the top bar of the screen, weeks are presented by their numbers within the year (Uke 51 - the 51 week of the year) and dates:

  • Tap a day to see the menu for this day. Middags Husen allows ordering for Monday - Thursday (Mandag - Torsdag)

  • The next screen is the menu available for the selected day. There are 2 dishes - first course and main course

    Tap on a dish you like

  • When on the Ordering screen, the following options are available for you:

    • Antall / Portions - the desired number of portions. 0, 1, 1.5, etc., up to 9.5 portions. The total summ / Totalsum per order is calculated dynamically when you select the number of portions.

    • Pick-up time - the time when you are going to take away your order. Time range available - from 15:00 to 18:00.

    • City / Poststed - one of the cities of your neighbourhood available from the list, where the Middags Huset orphan is located.

    • Address / Adresse - the address of the Middags Huset spot, where you are going to take away your order from

  • When everything is set up, tap the READY TO ORDER / FERTIG MED BESTILLINGEN button and then - YES, CONFIRM ORDER / JA, BEKREFT BESTILLING button.

The order will appear on the Home screen.

Repeat the above steps for the other days. When ready, proceed to Payment.
