HEALTHY, LOCAL AND EASY!

Middags Huset makes real Norwegian healthy dinners that you order weekly on our app, select daytime when you want to pick up your dinner - and the best part is that you can pick it up in your neighbourhood! This saves you time. And your family gets healthy and good dinners - every day!

General info

Middags Huset provides ability to order meals in Norway for every afternoon of the next week until 17:00 Friday this week.

The schedule for 4 days (Monday - Thursday) is displayed on the Home screen and shows the order-per-day information. Tap on a day leads to the corresponding Menu screen.

The menu contains 2 dishes - first and second courses. Tap on a dish box leads to the Order screen.

On the Order screen, the user can select the number of portions (from 1 to 9.5), specify convenient pick-up daytime, edit the order, confirm the order.


Features

Login with Facebook / Demo view

You can skip registration and see the fully-functional application using your Facebook login or just by tapping the SE MENYEN / SEE MENU button on the login screen. But ordering is available only for registered users.

Ordering dinners for the next week

Middags Huset is designed for ordering food for every day of the following week. Don't miss the opportunity and order food until 17:00 Friday current week.

Payment with a credit card directly from the app

The application has an integrated payment system, that makes payment for your orders timely and handy

Notifications

You won't forget to make an order for the next week or to pick up the today order due to informative automatic push-notifications.

Enhanced admin panel

The enhanced admin panel provides full control of the app. Customers, dishes, orders, notifications, etc., can be managed with no effort.
