Organize, share and discuss your items to fix at Fixlist!

Fixlist is designed for:

  • Creating lists of things / places to fix

  • Adding photos, documents, any other kind of descriptive information about what should be fixed

  • Adding participants to fixlists

  • Discussing, consulting, advising, simply chatting on a fixlist item

  • Organizing fixlists, items, participants, ideas


Features

Organize

Create as many items as you need and group them into fixlists. Choose categories for your fixlists. Add description, photos, attachments to items.

Discuss

Every item has a chat. Discuss the fixing process, ideas, suggestions, etc. You are the boss!

Share

Add participants to fixlists - people from Facebook you may know, registered Fixlist users, or you can use e-mail addresses to invite people to take part in discussion and the fixing process. Just share a fixlist, so as anyone can join

Get Inspired

Get relevant suggestions from the Internet based on your fixlist categories: furniture, appliences, materials, etc. Fixlist Inspo will gather all the needed products for you.
