Below is the list of notifications and cases when they are sent:

Weekly notifications

  • You should order food before 22:00 tonight.
    Du må bestille mat før klokken 22:00 i kveld.

    The notification is sent on Sundays at 20:00. It is not sent to a user in the given time if the user has at least one order for the next week.

  • Remember to order food for next week!
    Husk å bestille mat for neste uke!

    The notification is sent every Saturday at 14:00. It is not sent to a user in the given time if the user has at least one order for the next week.

Daily notifications

  • Remember to pick up the food at Middagshuset
    Husk å hente maten hos Middagshuset

    The notification is sent everyday at 11:00.

  • Remember to pick up the food at Middagshuset in 15 minutes
    Husk å hente maten på Middagshuset om 15 minutter!

    The notification is sent everyday 15 minutes before pick up time.

In addition to predefined notifications, the administrator can notify users manually from the admin panel with a random text and anytime.
